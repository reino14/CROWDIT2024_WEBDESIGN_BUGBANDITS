About Us

Bug Bandit was founded by three IT students from IPB University Reino, Sulthan,and Uzma who shared a love for hiking and the great outdoors. After numerous treks through Indonesia's diverse terrains, they noticed a gap in the market for affordable, high-quality hiking shoes. Inspired by their experiences, they decided to create a brand that combines durability, comfort, and style to enhance every hiker's journey.

Mission

Driven by their passion for nature and commitment to sustainability, Bug Bandit aims to provide footwear that supports adventurous spirits while preserving the environment. Each pair of shoes is designed with the modern explorer in mind, ensuring that every step taken is a step towards a more connected and conscious world. Join us in our journey to make every hike a memorable adventure

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading brand in the outdoor footwear industry, known for our exceptional products and commitment to sustainability. We aspire to inspire and equip adventurers worldwide, fostering a community that values exploration, environmental stewardship, and a deep connection to the natural world.
